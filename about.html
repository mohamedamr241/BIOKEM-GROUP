About BIOKEM

Est 2004

Our Story

BIOKEM was started in 2004 through a partnership with an Italian company. We aim to provide excellent cleaning services to hotel departments, focusing on hygiene. Our commitment comes from understanding the benefits of using natural substances, which are better for the environment than chemical alternatives. BIOKEM is proud to be a leader in using natural substances for cleaning. We prioritize the safety of our workers by using protective measures and proper equipment.

  • Our process is effective
  • Our process reduces industrial waste
  • Our process is protecting the environment

BIOKEM is committed to safety, efficiency, and environmental preservation. Our range of products provides practical solutions for diverse cleaning challenges, leveraging advanced technologies for seamless cleaning. Embodying our slogan, "Cleanliness is Our Talent," we confidently exceed client expectations.
